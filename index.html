You escaped!

Type "start" and hit Enter to begin.

Enter a command into the text box, and then press enter to proceed.
To move in a direction, enter "move (direction)" - e.g. "move north".
To talk to a character in a room, enter "talk".
Sometimes a character might ask you a question. To answer, enter "answer (your answer)" - answers will be a, b, c or d.
All commands should be no more than two words.
